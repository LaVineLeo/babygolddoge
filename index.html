    What is burning…?
    ✚

    Token burning is the process of permanently removing a portion of tokens from circulation, thereby reducing the total supply. This is performed by automatically sending 5% of the tokens of each transaction to the burn wallet address. As a result, we make the burn wallet the largest holder of our tokens. This is very useful for our investors, because the burn wallet can never be accessed and therefore all these Tangyuan tokens can never be accessed and sold. This will help increase the price and value of our tokens, greatly rewarding our holders!
